Project Requirements Summary

This system is designed for a single-office location with a flexible desk model and a total of 60 available seats across 6 modular tables, each seating 10 (two team rows of 5).

  • Total seat capacity: 60 desks — 6 tables × 10 seats
  • Flexible seating arrangement with admin-controlled Work From Home (WFH) adjustment
  • Team zones by table row:
    • Table 1 — BKT & AMEX 1
    • Table 2 — Art
    • Table 3 — Studio
    • Table 4 — AMEX 2 & EBCO
    • Table 5 — UnderArmour & Nat GEO
    • Table 6 — VYOMA & Finance/HR
  • Admins can assign, release, recolour and delete seats from the visual layout
  • Meeting room seats are excluded

The app includes:

  • Live seat map with free / occupied / reserved status
  • Employee directory with desk and department filtering
  • Admin controls for seat management and employee data
  • Requirement summary page for client discovery data
