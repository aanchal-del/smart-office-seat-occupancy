Project Requirements Summary

This system is designed for a single-office location with a flexible desk model and a total of 56 available seats across 6 modular tables.

  • Total staff: 66
  • Total seat capacity: 56 desks
  • Flexible seating arrangement with admin-controlled Work From Home (WFH) adjustment
  • Reserved seats for HR, IT, and Finance in Table 6
  • Separate zones: Client Servicing (Table 1), Creative Art (Table 2), Copywriting (Table 3), Films (Table 4), Studio (Table 5), Digital & Support (Table 6)
  • Meeting room seats are excluded

The app includes:

  • Live seat map with free / occupied / reserved status
  • Employee directory with desk and department filtering
  • Admin controls for seat management and employee data
  • Requirement summary page for client discovery data
